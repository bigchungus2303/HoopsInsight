# NBA Player Performance Predictor ‚Äì Project Specification & Execution Plan

## üéØ Purpose
A data-driven application that fetches NBA player statistics and models **regression-to-mean** behavior using both descriptive statistics and probabilistic analysis.  
The goal is to estimate the **cool-off probability** (inverse-likelihood of repeating high performances) across points, assists, rebounds, and 3-pointers ‚Äî adjusted for player career phase, seasonal normalization, and fatigue/load effects.

### 5. Inverse-Frequency Probability Model

**Core Formula:**
\[
P_{inv}(x) = 1 - f(x), \quad f(x) = \frac{1}{N}\sum I[\text{stat}_i ‚â• t]
\]

**Adjustments:**
1. **Career Phase Weighting**
   - Early ‚Üí rising trend (recent seasons weighted higher)  
   - Peak ‚Üí stable mean (balanced weighting)  
   - Late ‚Üí decline (stronger exponential decay)  
   \[
   w_i = e^{-Œª (T - t_i)}
   \]

2. **Seasonal Normalization (z-score)**
   \[
   z = \frac{x - Œº_{season}}{œÉ_{season}}
   \]

3. **Dynamic Player Thresholds**
   \[
   \text{thresholds} = \{Œº, Œº+œÉ, Œº+2œÉ, Œº+3œÉ\}
   \]

4. **Fatigue / Load Curve**
   - Rolling 10-game mean vs long-term mean  
   - If current > mean +1œÉ ‚Üí regression likelihood ‚Üë

5. **Injury / Minutes-Played Filter**
   - Declining minutes = lower sustain probability

6. **Non-Stationarity Handling**
   - Recency weighting keeps responsiveness while retaining long-term variance
